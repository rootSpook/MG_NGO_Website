HELP

infoAdmin Account Overview

As an Admin, you have full access to the structural content of the website. This includes the association's foundational information, legal documents, financial data, and governance records. Changes you make here are reflected directly on the public website.

editEditing MG Information

Navigate to MG Information to update the association's name, mission statement, vision, and about section. All fields support plain text. Click Save Changes to publish updates immediately.

gavelManaging Bylaws & Reports

Use the Bylaws page to upload and manage official statutes and regulations. Use Reports for annual activity and financial transparency reports. Supported file formats: PDF, DOC, DOCX. You can mark documents as Active or Archived.

account_balanceUpdating IBAN Data

The IBAN Data page controls the donation account information displayed on the website. Always double-check the IBAN number before saving — incorrect information can disrupt donor transactions.

webHomepage Slider & Slogans

Go to Homepage Content to manage the hero image slider and rotating slogans. You can reorder slides using the arrow buttons, and toggle slogans between Active and Inactive.

groupsBoard Members & Supporters

Add, edit, or remove board members from the Board Members page. Manage partner and sponsor logos on the Supporters page. Supporter logos appear in a grid on the public website.

lockAccount & Security

The Admin account has the highest privilege level in the CMS. Keep your credentials secure and do not share your login with others. For password resets or account issues, contact the system administrator.
